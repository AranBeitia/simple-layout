Character

Aku Aku

A LITTLE BIT ABOUT ME

Aku Aku is the spirit of an ancient witch doctor encased in a driftwood mask. During Crash's missions to stop Doctor Cortex, he scattered copies of himself throughout his travels in an effort to aid him on his mission.

In the first game and Crash Bandicoot 2: Cortex Strikes Back, he is a background character that helps Crash. He begins to play a major role in the "Crash Bandicoot: Warped" series, in which his evil twin brother Uka Uka shows up when the ruins of the Cortex space station crash into Earth and free Uka Uka, thus Aku Aku tells Crash and Coco Bandicoot the story of how he locked Uka Uka in an underground prison many years ago. He then gives the two of them the task of gathering Crystals that are scattered throughout time and keeping them out. from the hands of Uka Uka and Doctor Cortex During Crash's final fight against Doctor Neo Cortex, Aku Aku defends himself from Uka Uka's attacks while the fight is in session.
